Sauna Tip 1

Hydration:

Since saunas cause sweating, it's crucial to drink water to replenish fluids and prevent dehydration.

Sauna Tip 2

Gradual Acclimation

Beginners should start with shorter sauna sessions to allow their body to adjust to the heat.

Sauna Tip 3

Safety First

Pay attention to how your body feels and leave the sauna if you experience any discomfort.
